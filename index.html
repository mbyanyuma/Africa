ANCIENT AFRICA

Welcome to my website! My name is Moses Mulisa Byanyuma. This website is about ancient African history in relation to Africa in our present day. I have chosen do this because not much is known about African history to the world, including 21st century generation Africans. The purpose of this site is to educate and hopefully empower the reader by presenting them information that spans over 6 thousand years.

Some of the information that will be conveyed in the subsequent pages are as follows:

  • African oral history and why African history is not well-known in the first place. In addition, the inclusion and subsequent exclusion of written history regarding ancient Africa.
  • Ancient Egypt. Now before I can present detailed information on other regions of Africa it is imperative to start in ancient Egypt as it was the most advanced region in the world during ancient times. Furthermore, information will be presented about Nubia (modern Sudan) and how this region has more pyramids than in Egypt.
  • Present scientific evidence proves that human civilization began in Africa. Therefore, i will provide clarity to the reader by providing information on the origin of African civilization, which began around lake Victoria and along the river Nile, this is important because the conditions in this region paved the way for ancient Egypt and Nubia to prosper.
  • Tribalization and the balkanization of big states into many smaller ones, this will follow up on the second page and explain further as to why Africa today has a cultural unity yet is so divided among countless ethnic tribes.
  • Intermediary between ancient Africa and 21st century Africa, specifically on the scramble for Africa and the independence movements of the 60s.
  • Information about the current 54 nation-states in the continent, specifically on their current cultural and sociopolitical status.
  • Predictions on the near future of Africa.
